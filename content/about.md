Modern History UK is a collection of history resources by Paul Waring.

I'm a [freelance web developer](https://www.phpdeveloper.org.uk) based in Manchester, UK. Although my background is in computing, after my first Computer Science degree I decided it would be fun to spend a year studying Ancient History. I'd been interested in the subject for a long time but couldn't study it at GCSE or A level, but I was accepted onto the MA in Ancient World Studies course at the University of Manchester. Although I've only studied Ancient History, I am interested in most time periods, especially World War II and the Napoleonic Wars.

I'm a member of the [History group](https://warrington.u3asite.uk/u3a_groups/history/) of [Warrington U3A](https://warrington.u3asite.uk/), which my late grandfather helped to start, and I usually give two talks a year. I also help with organising the group. We currently meet once a month (except July and August) and new members are always welcome.
